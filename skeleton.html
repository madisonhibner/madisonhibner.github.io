About Me

Howdy! My name is Madison Hibner. I'm currently a junior at The University of Wisconsin-Madison studying strategic communications with certificates in digital media studies and entreprenuership...exciting things! Throughout the later years of my childhood I discovered my passion for creative content creation and creative expression. Ideally after graduation I would love to work within an advertising agency although, I'm still learning more about the specific role for me.

My chilhood was rooted right outside of Madison, WI in the suburban town of Cottage Grove. I loved growing up in such a traditional neighborhood alongside both my parents and two younger brothers. I was able to live a beautiful, simple, and fun childhood without ever having to grow up too fast. Now, at the age of twenty I look forward to extending my reach beyond the Madison-area with the hopes of exploring more this country has to offer post-graduation.

The intention of this website is to provide a quick, yet visual representation of my work thus far in my career. My fabulous education at UW-Madison has provided me numerous opporunities to develop my professional skills via classes. In result, I have accumulated a variety of work including photography, videography, written articles, and creative graphics. I hope you are able to appreciate the thought, skill development, and creative emphasis dedicated to my work as much as I do!
